Welcome to the Department of Computer Science at Ramanujan College, University of Delhi. Our department is committed to academic excellence and innovation in computer science education and research.

About the Department

The Department of Computer Science was established with the aim of producing highly skilled professionals in the field of Information Technology and Computer Applications. We offer undergraduate programs that focus on theoretical foundations as well as practical skills.

Courses Offered

• B.Sc. (Hons) Computer Science
• B.A. Programme with Computer Applications
• Certificate Courses in Data Science, AI & Web Development

Our Vision

To foster a vibrant learning environment that nurtures innovation, research, and leadership in computer science and technology.
